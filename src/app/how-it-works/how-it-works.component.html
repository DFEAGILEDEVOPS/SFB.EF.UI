Introduction

  1. The efficiency metric

    The metric provides an indication of a schools efficiency compared to 49 statistically similar schools based on certain characteristics.

  2. How it is calculated

    We divide a school's progress (KS2 or Progress 8) by their per pupil expenditure which gives us a score which allows us to rank the 50 schools. KS2 progress or Progress 8 / Expenditure per pupil = Efficiency metric score

  3. What you should do with the information

    It is intended to be used as a prompt for discussion at and between schools about how a school can improve the way they allocate resource to have a positive impact on pupil progress.

It is not an accountability measure. i.e. schools should not be held to account for their efficiency metric ranking.
